OUR GAMES

Proven champions across multiple competitive titles

Honor Of Kings

Tactical FPS

A fast-paced, 5v5 multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by TiMi Studio Group and published by Tencent Games. Players choose from a diverse roster of heroes—each with unique abilities—and battle across dynamic maps to destroy enemy towers and the base. Known for its strategic gameplay, smooth controls, and strong competitive scene, it’s one of the most popular mobile games worldwide.

Valorant

MOBA Strategy

A free-to-play 5v5 tactical first-person shooter developed by Riot Games. Combining precise gunplay with unique agent abilities, players engage in fast-paced rounds where strategy, teamwork, and sharp aim are key. With a blend of traditional FPS mechanics and hero-based gameplay, VALORANT has become a staple in competitive gaming and esports.

Counter-Strike 2

MOBA Strategy

A free-to-play 5v5 MOBA developed by Riot Games for mobile and console. It brings the core gameplay of League of Legends to a faster, streamlined format with shorter matches, touch-friendly controls, and a redesigned map. Players choose from a wide roster of champions to battle in strategic team-based combat, aiming to destroy the enemy Nexus.
